About this tool

Built on research, not guesswork

HalluDetect's detection patterns are derived from a Systematic Literature Review (SLR) of 6 peer-reviewed papers on AI-generated code hallucination. Every flagged issue maps to a hallucination category documented in published research — including CodeHalu (Tian et al., 2024), HALLUCODE (Liu et al., 2024), and Zhang et al. (ISSTA 2024).
